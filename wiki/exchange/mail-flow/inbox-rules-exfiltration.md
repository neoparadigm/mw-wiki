Title: Prompt Engineering Pro: Mastering the Art of Communicating with AI in 2025

Overview:
In the rapidly evolving world of data science and artificial intelligence, prompt engineering has become an essential skill due to the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) into various industries. This entry will discuss what prompt engineering is, its importance, and provide tips for crafting effective prompts to get the best possible results from AI.

Key Concepts:
1. Prompt Engineering
2. Large Language Models (LLMs)
3. Instruction
4. Context
5. Input/Question
6. Output Format
7. System Prompt
8. Precise but concise prompts
9. Providing context
10. Using clear structures
11. Sequential Thought Process Prompting
12. Generated Insight Prompt

Configuration:
- Understand the basics of a prompt, including instruction, context, input/question, output format, and system prompt.
- Be concise yet precise when crafting prompts.
- Provide necessary context to help AI understand the query better.
- Use clear structures like bullet points or numbered lists to help AI organize information.
- For advanced techniques, encourage step-by-step problem solving (Sequential Thought Process Prompting) and generate insights (Generated Insight Prompt).

Common Pitfalls:
1. Vague or unclear prompts leading to inaccurate responses.
2. Lack of context resulting in misinterpretation by the AI.
3. Not using clear structures, making it difficult for AI to organize information effectively.
4. Overcomplicating prompts, which can confuse the AI and lead to incorrect results.
